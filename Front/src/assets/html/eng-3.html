Duration is how long the note is held for. Here are the five most common ones:

A quarter note is one beat.

A half note is two beats, equivalent to two quarter notes.

A whole note is four beats, equivalent to four quarter notes.

An Eighth note is half of a quarter note.

A sixteenth note is half of an eighth note.

As you can see, adding a flag halves the duration. You can keep adding flags to shorten the note.
