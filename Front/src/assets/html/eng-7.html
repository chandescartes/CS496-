We can play the same note softly or loudly and it will sound entirely different. Dynamics is how loud or quiet the sound is.

Here are some common dynamic levels:

pp p mp mf f ff
very soft soft medium soft medium loud loud very loud

Changes in dynamics can also be marked anywhere in the music. For example, crescendo, or cresc., means to get gradually louder, whereas diminuendo, or dim., means to get gradually softer.

Emphasis on individual notes is placed with an accent or the sforzando (sf.) mark. This note should be played significantly more strongly than others.
